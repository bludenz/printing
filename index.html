About Us

Welcome to TurboDiesel Labs — where we aim to turn your concepts into reality. Founded by a high-achieving engineering student with a passion for mechanics, CAD design, and innovative fabrication methods, TurboDiesel Labs was built on the belief that powerful ideas deserve to be brought to life with accuracy, care, and technical excellence. We offer a full suite of 3D printing solutions designed to support every stage of your final outcome requirements. From conceptual design to cost-effective prototyping and high-quality fabrication, we are equipped to handle a wide range of projects—whether you're looking for a toy, a replacement part or wanting to design a product of your own.

At TurboDiesel Labs, we understand that no two projects are the same. That’s why we take a tailored approach, working closely with you to understand your goals and deliver customised solutions that meet your specifications, timeline, and budget. Our prototyping services are offered at a reduced cost, enabling innovators and small teams to iterate quickly without compromising on quality. Utilising advanced 3D printing technologies and materials, we ensure exceptional surface finishes, dimensional accuracy, and structural integrity across all builds. Our capabilities range from functional mechanical parts and detailed architectural models to complex artistic pieces and custom hardware.

But we’re more than just a printing service—we’re a partner in innovation. Our commitment to quality, reliability, and continuous improvement is at the core of everything we do. Every layer we print is a step toward the future, helping our clients solve real-world problems and push the boundaries of what’s possible. Whether you're looking to prototype a new invention, replace a hard-to-find part, or explore the creative potential of additive manufacturing, TurboDiesel Labs is here to support you—with the tools, talent, and dedication to bring your ideas to life.

Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We primarily accept .STL, .OBJ, and .3MF files. If you have another format, please contact us to discuss compatibility.

The filament that I want is not listed?

If there is a filament that you would like to select for your print, and it is not listed on our website, please contact us and we can special order filament just for you. Note, this will result in an additional charge.

How long does a print take?

Print times vary greatly depending on the model's size, complexity, chosen material, and desired print quality. We'll provide an estimated turnaround time with your quote.

Can you help with design?

Yes! While our primary service is printing, we can offer design services as well.
