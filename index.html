Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We primarily accept .STL, .OBJ, and .3MF files. If you have another format, please contact us to discuss compatibility.

How long does a print take?

Print times vary greatly depending on the model's size, complexity, chosen material, and desired print quality. We'll provide an estimated turnaround time with your quote.

Can you help with design?

While our primary service is printing, we can offer basic advice or recommend design resources if you need assistance with your model.
