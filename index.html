How to Find Models & Place an Order

Step 1: Finding Your Model

You can find pre-designed 3D models on various online repositories. Some popular sites include:

  • **Thingiverse:** A vast library of free, user-created designs.
  • **Printables:** Another excellent resource with a focus on printable designs and contests.
  • **MyMiniFactory:** Curated selection of high-quality 3D printable files.
  • **Cults 3D:** Features both free and paid designs, often for more niche or detailed models.

When searching, look for models in common 3D printing file formats like **.STL** or **.OBJ**. Make sure the model is suitable for 3D printing and note its approximate dimensions if possible.

Step 2: Placing Your Order

Once you have your model (or a clear idea of what you want designed), placing an order with us is simple:

  1. **Contact Us:** Send an email to admin@bludenz.dev with your request.
  2. **Attach Your Model:** If you have a file, attach it. If you need a custom design, describe your idea in detail.
  3. **Specify Requirements:** Let us know your preferred filament type and color (refer to our list above), and any specific dimensions or strength requirements.
  4. **Receive Your Quote:** We'll review your request and provide a detailed quote including material cost, print time, and any design fees.
  5. **Approve & Print:** Once you approve the quote and make a payment, we'll start printing your design!

For custom designs, we'll work closely with you through the design process to ensure your vision is accurately captured before printing.
